VibeXP is your personal AI productivity platform that helps you efficiently use AI tools and maximize your productivity. Transform scattered AI outputs into organized, searchable knowledge bases while maintaining persistent context across all your conversations.

VibeXP is **open source** — you can [self-host](/user-guide/self-hosting/) your own instance on your own domain, with no paid tiers. See [Open Source](/user-guide/open-source/) for the license model and how to get involved.

## What is VibeXP?

VibeXP provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed to enhance your AI workflow:

- **Prompt Management** - Create, organize, and reuse AI prompt templates
- **Artifacts Management** - Organize your AI-generated content into a searchable knowledge base
- **Memory Management** - Intelligent AI context persistence across conversations
- **MCP Server Integration** - Direct integration with Claude Code, Cursor, and VS Code
- **AI Agents** - Connect and automate with A2A-compatible AI agents
- **API Keys** - Secure access for your favorite development tools

## Why Choose VibeXP?

### Save Time
Stop rewriting the same prompts. Build reusable templates with variable placeholders and get consistent, high-quality results every time.

### Never Lose Content
Automatically capture and organize AI-generated code, documentation, and reports. Find anything instantly with powerful search and filtering.

### Maintain Context
Store project details, coding standards, and preferences once. Your AI conversations always have the right context without repeating yourself.

### Seamless Integration
Connect your AI tools directly to your VibeXP account through our MCP server. Access your prompts, artifacts, and memories from Claude Code, Cursor, or VS Code.
